Computer Science & Engnr. Dept., UOI

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Ioannina, was founded in 1990 as a Computer Science department and in June 2013 was upgraded and renamed to Department of Computer Science & Engineering.

The Parallel Processing Group (PPG)

PPG ' s main research interests lie in the area of parallel and distributed systems, at the theoretical, architectural, systems, analysis and application levels.

The Computer Systems Support Group

The Systems Support Group provides assistant work in the management of Computer Science and Engineering Department ' s computing systems.

OMPi OpenMP C compiler by PPG

OMPi is a lightweight, open source OpenMP compiler and runtime system for C. It sports an architecture that allows experimentation with a multitude of threading libraries while offering highly competitive performance.

Simple Pass Vault

Android Application

Simple Password Vault helps you store the emails, usernames, websites and passwords that you may need while signing in to different sites, bank accounts, PCs etc. This application does not connect to the internet, so your data always stays inside an encrypted database in your device.
